## Why

The OSS Growth Index is migrating to oscar.dev under the brand name **Oscar** (Open Source Supabase Commit Analytical Ranking). The current landing page has no Oscar identity — the new domain needs a landing experience that introduces the brand, explains the acronym, and makes the Oscar statuette SVG the visual centrepiece.

## What Changes

- Introduce the Oscar name, acronym, and brand identity to the landing page
- Display the Oscar statuette SVG as a hero element with a delightful presentation
- Write and display the full acronym expansion: "Open Source Supabase Commit Analytical Ranking"
- Adapt the page copy and tone to the Oscar brand (while keeping the coming-soon structure)

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `oscar-hero`: The Oscar statuette SVG displayed as a prominent, animated hero element — the visual anchor of the page
- `oscar-acronym`: Display of the full acronym expansion, designed to feel like a reveal or poem rather than a dry definition

### Modified Capabilities

*(none — this is a new brand layer on top of the existing landing structure)*
